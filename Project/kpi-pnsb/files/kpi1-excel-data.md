## Key Finding — B2 Resolution (Tier Score Formula)

Derived from cross-referencing `Pencapaian Sebenar` vs tier targets vs `Skor` across all 14 KPI rows:

| Tier Achieved | Pencapaian Sebenar (Score Index) |
|---|---|
| Below Threshold (Ambangan) | **0** |
| At Threshold (Ambangan) | **60** |
| At Meet Target (Setuju) | **80** |
| At / Above Stretched (Lebihan) | **100** |

**Skor Formula**: `Skor = (Pencapaian Sebenar / 100) × Jumlah Kecil Pemberat`

**Verified examples**:
- SOP row: achieved 4 SOP = Threshold → 60. Score = (60/100) × 5% = 3.0% ✓
- Safety activities: achieved 7 = Meet Target → 80. Score = (80/100) × 5% = 4.0% ✓
- EDMS: uploaded 14,723 docs > Stretched 12,000 → capped at 100. Score = (100/100) × 5% = 5.0% ✓

**Score cap**: Hard cap at 100 — exceeding Stretched does NOT push score above 100.
